<p><p>A 3dB hybrid, also known as a 3 decibel hybrid or directional coupler, is an electronic component used in telecommunications systems to couple electromagnetic power from one port to another. It provides a balanced power splitting and combining function, allowing for the transmission of signals in both directions with minimal loss of power. The name "3dB" comes from the fact that this type of hybrid device introduces a 3dB insertion loss, distributing the power evenly between two ports.</p><p>The future outlook of the 3dB hybrid market is promising, with a projected CAGR of 9.70% during the forecasted period.
