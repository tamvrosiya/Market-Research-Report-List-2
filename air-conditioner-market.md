<p><ul><li>Invariable Frequency Air Condition</li><li>Frequency Conversion Air Conditioning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of air conditioner markets: invariable frequency air conditioners and frequency conversion air conditioners. Invariable frequency air conditioners have a fixed speed compressor, which means they run at a constant speed and are less energy efficient. On the other hand, frequency conversion air conditioners use variable speed compressors that adjust according to cooling needs, making them more energy efficient and cost-effective in the long run. This makes frequency conversion air conditioners more popular in the market today.</p></p>
