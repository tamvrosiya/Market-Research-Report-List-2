<p><p>Airborne System Surveillance Radar (ASR) is a radar system used on aircraft to provide continuous surveillance of a designated area. ASR provides real-time monitoring of land, sea, and air targets and offers advanced capabilities to detect and track objects. </p><p>The Airborne System Surveillance Radar market is projected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. One such trend is the integration of ASR with other systems and platforms to enhance its capabilities and performance. Another trend is the development of multi-mode radars, which can switch between different radar modes based on the mission requirements.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology, such as the use of active electronically scanned array (AESA) technology and software-defined radar, are driving market growth.
