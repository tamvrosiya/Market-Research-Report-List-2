<p><p>ANSI Process Pumps come in two main market types: Sealless and Sealed. Sealless pumps do not have traditional seals, reducing the risk of leaks and maintenance costs. They are ideal for handling hazardous or corrosive fluids. Sealed pumps, on the other hand, have mechanical seals to prevent leaks and are commonly used for general industrial applications. Both types of pumps comply with ANSI (American National Standards Institute) standards for performance and dimensions, ensuring high quality and reliability in various industrial processes.</p></p>
