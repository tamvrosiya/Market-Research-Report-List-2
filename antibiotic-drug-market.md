<p><p>The antibiotic drug market consists of various types of drugs that help in combating bacterial infections. Penicillins, such as amoxicillin and ampicillin, are the most commonly prescribed antibiotics and work by interfering with the formation of bacterial cell walls. Aminoglycosides, like gentamicin and streptomycin, inhibit protein synthesis in bacteria. Tetracyclines, such as doxycycline and minocycline, disrupt protein synthesis by binding to the bacterial ribosome. Chloramphenicol acts by inhibiting protein synthesis and is used for serious bacterial infections. Macrolides, like azithromycin and clarithromycin, interfere with the production of essential bacterial proteins. Other types of antibiotics in the market include fluoroquinolones, sulfonamides, and cephalosporins.</p></p>
