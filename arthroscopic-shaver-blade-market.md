<p><p>The Arthroscopic Shaver Blade market is classified into two main types: Disposable Arthroscopic Shaver Blade and Reprocessed Arthroscopic Shaver Blade. The Disposable Arthroscopic Shaver Blade refers to a one-time use blade that is discarded after each surgical procedure, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of infection. In contrast, Reprocessed Arthroscopic Shaver Blades are carefully cleaned and sterilized, allowing them to be reused multiple times, thereby reducing costs for hospitals and healthcare facilities. Both types serve the purpose of cutting and removing tissue during arthroscopic surgeries but differ in terms of single-use or multiple-use capabilities.</p></p>
