<p><p>Asbestos removal equipment market can be broadly classified into two types: asbestos abatement equipment and asbestos removal equipment. Asbestos abatement equipment is used during the process of controlling and minimizing the release of asbestos fibers into the environment. It includes specialized tools such as containment units, negative air machines, and HEPA vacuums. On the other hand, asbestos removal equipment is used for the physical removal and disposal of asbestos-containing materials. This equipment includes protective clothing, respirators, gloves, and other tools necessary for safe removal operations. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient removal of asbestos.</p></p>
