<p><p>Biomedical adhesives are used to bond biological tissues or medical devices in healthcare applications. They provide strong, durable adhesion and are often used in wound closure and tissue bonding. Biomedical sealants, on the other hand, are used to seal or encapsulate medical devices to prevent leakage or contamination. They provide a flexible, watertight barrier and can be used in applications such as hemostasis and wound healing. Both biomedical adhesives and sealants play a crucial role in the healthcare industry in ensuring reliable and safe medical procedures.</p></p>
