Aramid, including Kevlar, is valued for its excellent resistance to heat, flame, and chemicals. Composite ceramic body armor combines ceramic plates with other materials for enhanced protection against projectiles. These diverse types of body armor cater to various needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
