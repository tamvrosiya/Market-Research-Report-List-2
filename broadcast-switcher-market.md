<p><p>The broadcast switcher market can be categorized into different types based on the input formats they support. HDMI input switchers are designed to handle high-definition multimedia interface signals, commonly used in consumer electronics. HD-SDI input switchers are capable of handling high-definition serial digital interface signals, mostly used in professional broadcasting. Other types of broadcast switchers encompass a wide range of input formats like composite video, component video, VGA, DVI, etc. These different market types cater to the varying needs of broadcasters, allowing them to source and switch between different input formats as required.</p></p>
