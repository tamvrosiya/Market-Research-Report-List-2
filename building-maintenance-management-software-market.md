<p><p>Building Maintenance Management Software (BMMS) is a platform that helps organizations efficiently manage and maintain their buildings and assets. There are two types of BMMS market: cloud-based and on-premises.</p><p>Cloud-based BMMS operates on remote servers, enabling users to access the software and data using an internet connection. This type offers scalability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of access, as updates and maintenance are handled by the service provider.</p><p>On-premises BMMS is installed and operates on the organization's own servers. It offers more control, as data is stored locally, but requires higher upfront costs, maintenance efforts, and IT infrastructure.</p></p>
