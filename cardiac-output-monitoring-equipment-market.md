<p><strong>The Cardiac Output Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Minimally Invasive</li><li>Non-Invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiac output monitoring equipment includes invasive, minimally invasive, and non-invasive types. Invasive equipment involves placing a catheter into the patient's arteries or veins to directly measure cardiac output. Minimally invasive devices utilize external sensors or cuffs to estimate cardiac output. Non-invasive equipment measures cardiac output without the need for any invasive procedures, typically using technologies like echocardiography or electrical impedance. These different types of equipment provide healthcare professionals with various options to accurately measure cardiac output and assist in the diagnosis and management of cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
