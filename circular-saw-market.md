<p><p>The circular saw market can be classified into two types: corded circular saws and cordless circular saws. Corded circular saws are power tools that operate by connecting to a power source through a cord. These saws offer a consistent and uninterrupted power supply, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, cordless circular saws are battery-powered, providing mobility and convenience. These saws are ideal for locations without electricity or when working in tight spaces.
