<p><p>A clapper valve is a type of check valve that allows the flow of fluid in only one direction, preventing backflow. It consists of a hinged flap or disc which opens when the fluid flows in the intended direction and closes under reverse flow. Clapper valves are commonly used in a wide range of applications, including oil and gas, water treatment, chemical processing, and wastewater treatment.</p><p>The clapper valve market is witnessing steady growth, driven by factors such as increasing demand for efficient flow control systems and rising investments in infrastructure development.
