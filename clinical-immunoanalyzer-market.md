<p><strong>The Clinical Immunoanalyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CLIA</li><li>ELISA</li><li>RIA</li><li>FIA</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clinical immunoanalyzer market includes various types such as CLIA (Chemiluminescence Immunoassay), ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), RIA (Radioimmunoassay), FIA (Fluorescence Immunoassay), and others. CLIA utilizes chemiluminescent substrates for measuring antigens or antibodies. ELISA employs enzyme-linked antibodies to measure antigens, antibodies, or other biomarkers. RIA uses radiolabeled molecules to detect the presence of antigens or antibodies. FIA utilizes fluorescent labels for detection of antigens or antibodies. Other immunoanalyzer methods include immunofluorescence, immunoelectrophoresis, flow cytometry, and Western blotting. These techniques are widely used in clinical laboratories for diagnosing diseases and monitoring patient health.</p></p>
