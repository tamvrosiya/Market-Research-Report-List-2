<p><p>Clinical Trial Management System (CTMS) is a software that helps manage and streamline various aspects of clinical trials. There are two main types of CTMS markets: Enterprise CTMS and Site CTMS. </p><p>Enterprise CTMS refers to a system that caters to the needs of large organizations conducting multiple clinical trials across various sites. It offers comprehensive management features like study planning, resource allocation, budgeting, and reporting. </p><p>On the other hand, Site CTMS is designed for individual clinical trial sites to efficiently manage their specific trial operations. It focuses on patient recruitment, visit scheduling, data collection, and regulatory compliance. </p><p>Both types aim to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and compliance in clinical trial management, albeit catering to different levels of the trial operation.</p></p>
