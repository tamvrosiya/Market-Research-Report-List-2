<p><p>Closed vial access devices are used to prevent contamination and ensure the safe withdrawal of medications from vials. There are two types available in the market - with needle and needleless. With needle devices have a needle attached to the access port, allowing for direct extraction of medication. Needleless devices, on the other hand, use a spike or other mechanism to access the vial without the need for a needle. Both types offer advantages such as reducing the risk of needlestick injuries and maintaining a sterile environment during medication administration.</p></p>
