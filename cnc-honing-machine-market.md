<p><ul><li>Vertical Honing Machines</li><li>Horizonta Honing Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC honing machines are categorized into two main types: vertical honing machines and horizontal honing machines. Vertical honing machines are designed with a vertically oriented spindle and honing tool, allowing for efficient honing of parts in a downward direction. On the other hand, horizontal honing machines feature a horizontally oriented spindle and honing tool, enabling the honing process to be carried out in a horizontal direction. Both types of machines offer precision and high-quality honing capabilities but differ in their orientation and the direction of honing.</p></p>
