<p><p>The CNC Jig Grinder market is divided into two types: Automatic and Semi-automatic. Automatic jig grinders are fully automated machines that can perform grinding operations without human intervention. They have advanced features like in-process measurement, automatic tool changers, and can work continuously for long periods. On the other hand, semi-automatic jig grinders require some manual intervention and supervision during the grinding process. They offer more flexibility and control to the operator but may require more expertise. Both types cater to different needs and applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
