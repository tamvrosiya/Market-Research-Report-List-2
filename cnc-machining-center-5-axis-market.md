<p><strong>The CNC Machining Center (5-axis) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Machining Centers</li><li>Horizontal Machining Centers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNC Machining Center market can be classified into two types: Vertical Machining Centers (VMC) and Horizontal Machining Centers (HMC). </p><p>Vertical Machining Centers are designed with the spindle mounted vertically, allowing for the cutting tool to move vertically along the Z-axis. This configuration is ideal for operations that require drilling or milling holes in a workpiece.</p><p>On the other hand, Horizontal Machining Centers have the spindle positioned horizontally, enabling the cutting tool to move horizontally along the X-axis. HMCs are suitable for operations that involve heavy machining or require high precision on multiple sides of a workpiece.</p></p>
