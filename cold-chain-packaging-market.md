<p><p>Cold chain packaging refers to the packaging systems and materials used to ensure the safe transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive products, such as perishable food, vaccines, and pharmaceuticals. There are two main types of cold chain packaging markets: single-use packaging and reusable packaging. Single-use packaging is designed for one-time use and is typically made of materials like expanded polystyrene or corrugated cardboard. Reusable packaging, on the other hand, is designed for multiple uses and is usually made of durable materials like molded plastic or stainless steel. Both types serve the purpose of maintaining the required temperature conditions to prevent spoilage or degradation of the products.</p></p>
