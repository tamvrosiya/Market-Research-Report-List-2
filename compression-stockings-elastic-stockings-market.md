<p><p>Compression stockings, also known as elastic stockings, come in two main market types: gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks provide graduated compression, tighter at the ankle and gradually looser as it moves up the leg, to improve blood flow and help prevent swelling. Anti-embolism socks are specifically designed to reduce the risk of blood clots during periods of immobility, such as after surgery. Both types of compression stockings offer medical benefits for various conditions related to circulation and are available in different styles and compression levels.</p></p>
