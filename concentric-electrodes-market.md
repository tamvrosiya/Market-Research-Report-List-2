<p><strong>The Concentric Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tungsten/Stainless Steel Core</li><li>Platinum/Iridium Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concentric electrodes are categorized into two types: tungsten/stainless steel core and platinum/iridium core. Tungsten/stainless steel core electrodes consist of a tungsten wire at the center, surrounded by a stainless steel tube. These electrodes provide high durability and are commonly used in high-temperature applications. On the other hand, platinum/iridium core electrodes have a platinum/iridium alloy wire at the center, offering excellent chemical stability and electrical conductivity. These electrodes are often utilized in biomedical research and various physiological measurements, ensuring accurate and reliable results.</p></p>
