<p><p>Cone Beam CT (CBCT) is a medical imaging technology that provides detailed three-dimensional (3D) images of dental and maxillofacial structures. It uses a cone-shaped X-ray beam and a flat-panel detector to capture multiple images from different angles, which are then reconstructed and displayed as a 3D image. CBCT offers several advantages over traditional CT scans, including lower radiation exposure, shorter scan times, and higher image resolution. It is commonly used in dentistry, orthodontics, endodontics, and oral and maxillofacial surgery.</p><p>The Cone Beam CT market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing prevalence of dental and oral diseases, rising demand for cosmetic dentistry, and advancements in CBCT technology are driving market growth.
