<p><p>Corrugated aluminum sheath cables have different market types, namely HV (high voltage) and EHV (extra high voltage) corrugated aluminum sheath cables. HV corrugated aluminum sheath cables are designed to transmit electricity at high voltage levels, typically ranging from 69 kV to 230 kV. On the other hand, EHV corrugated aluminum sheath cables are built to handle even higher voltage requirements, usually higher than 230 kV. Both types of cables are used in various applications, such as power transmission and distribution networks, to ensure efficient and reliable electricity transfer.</p></p>
