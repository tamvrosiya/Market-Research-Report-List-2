<p><ul><li>Single-chip Polishing</li><li>Batch Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crystalline silicon polishing equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to polish silicon wafers. There are two main types of this equipment: single-chip polishing and batch polishing. Single-chip polishing equipment is designed to polish individual silicon wafers one at a time, offering precise control and accuracy. On the other hand, batch polishing equipment can process multiple wafers simultaneously, increasing the production efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in achieving the desired smoothness and flatness of silicon wafers in various semiconductor applications.</p></p>
