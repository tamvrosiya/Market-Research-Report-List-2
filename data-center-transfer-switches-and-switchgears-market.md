<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Data Center Transfer Switches and Switchgears market comprises two main types: Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) and Static Transfer Switch (STS). ATS is used for transferring power supply from one source to another during electrical outages, ensuring uninterrupted power to critical equipment in data centers. It automatically detects power disruptions and seamlessly switches to a backup power source. On the other hand, STS transfers power between multiple sources without interruption, maximizing uptime and preventing downtime. These switches are vital for maintaining reliable power supply in data centers and protecting against potential losses or damages.</p></p>
