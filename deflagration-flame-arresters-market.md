<p><p>Deflagration flame arresters are safety devices used to prevent the propagation of flames and explosions in industrial settings. There are two main types of deflagration flame arresters: in-line and end-of-line. In-line flame arresters are installed within a pipeline and are designed to instantly extinguish any flames that may occur. On the other hand, end-of-line flame arresters are installed at the end of a pipeline or vessel and are used to prevent flames from entering or exiting. Both types are crucial in ensuring the safety of industrial operations.</p></p>
