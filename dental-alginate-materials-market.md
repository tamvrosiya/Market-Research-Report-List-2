<p><p>Dental alginate is a flexible material commonly used in dentistry to produce molds or impressions of teeth and gums. Dental alginate materials are commonly supplied as a powder that is mixed with water to create a viscous gel-like substance. Once the mixture is applied to the teeth and gums, it quickly solidifies into a mold that can be used for further dental procedures.</p><p>The dental alginate materials market is projected to experience substantial growth in the coming years.
