<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Dental Bender</li><li>Electric Dental Bender</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental benders are devices used in dentistry to shape orthodontic wires. There are two main types of dental benders: pneumatic dental benders and electric dental benders. </p><p>Pneumatic dental benders are powered by air pressure and are generally lighter and more portable. They offer precise control over the bending process and are ideal for small adjustments or delicate procedures.</p><p>Electric dental benders, on the other hand, are powered by electricity. They provide consistent force and speed, making them suitable for heavy-duty bending tasks. They are commonly used in larger dental clinics or laboratories where higher production is required.</p></p>
