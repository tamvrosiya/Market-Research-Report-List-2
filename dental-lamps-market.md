<p><p>The dental lamps market consists of two main types: LED dental lamps and halogen dental lamps. LED dental lamps use light-emitting diodes to produce bright and focused light, providing excellent visibility for dentists during procedures. They are energy-efficient, have a longer lifespan, and emit less heat. On the other hand, halogen dental lamps use halogen bulbs to provide illumination. They are more affordable but have a shorter lifespan and emit more heat. Dentists may choose between these two types depending on their specific requirements and budget.</p></p>
