<p><p>In the Desiccators market, two types are commonly found: Standard and Automatic. Standard desiccators are the traditional ones that require manual operation. They consist of a glass or plastic container with a removable lid and a drying agent, such as silica gel, inside. On the other hand, automatic desiccators are equipped with electronic controls and sensors to regulate the humidity level and maintain a dry environment automatically. They offer more convenience and efficiency compared to standard models. Both types serve the purpose of preserving moisture-sensitive items by creating a low-humidity environment inside the desiccator.</p></p>
