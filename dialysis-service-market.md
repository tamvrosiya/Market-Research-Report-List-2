<p><strong>The Dialysis Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dialysis service market is comprised of two main types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves the use of a machine to filter the blood outside the body, removing waste and excess fluids. It is typically performed at a dialysis center. Peritoneal dialysis, on the other hand, utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity to filter blood inside the body. It is often done at home with the help of a special fluid that is infused and drained from the abdominal cavity. Both methods help maintain proper kidney function in individuals with kidney failure.</p></p>
