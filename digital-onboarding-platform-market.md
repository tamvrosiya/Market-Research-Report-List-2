<p><p>The digital onboarding platform market can be classified into two types: cloud-based and web-based. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as users don't need to invest in infrastructure. On the other hand, web-based platforms are accessed directly through a web browser. Both types enable streamlined customer onboarding processes, but cloud-based solutions provide additional benefits in terms of accessibility and cost.</p></p>
