<p><p>The doctor blade system is widely used in various printing applications such as flexographic printing, intaglio printing, offset printing, and others. In intaglio printing, the doctor blade is used to clear excess ink from the non-image areas, improving print quality. In offset printing, the doctor blade helps keep the ink fountain roller clean, preventing ink contamination. These applications benefit from the efficient and reliable performance of doctor blade systems.</p></p>
