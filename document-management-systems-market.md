<p><p>Document Management Systems (DMS) are software solutions that enable organizations to create, store, manage, and retrieve their documents efficiently. They are available in different market types: </p><p>1. On-premise DMS: This type requires the installation and maintenance of the software on the user's local servers, allowing organizations to have complete control over their data and infrastructure.</p><p>2. Cloud-based DMS: This type operates on remote servers maintained by the DMS provider, offering online accessibility from any location, increased scalability, and reduced hardware investment.</p><p>3. Hybrid DMS: This type integrates both on-premise and cloud-based solutions, enabling organizations to store their critical data locally while benefiting from the cloud's flexibility and remote accessibility for non-sensitive information.</p></p>
