<p><p>Double suction pumps are centrifugal pumps that have an impeller with two entry points for the suction of fluid. This design helps to achieve a balanced hydraulic force and prevent axial thrust, resulting in smoother operation and extended pump life. They are commonly used in applications that require high flow rates and low NPSH (Net Positive Suction Head), such as water supply systems, HVAC systems, and irrigation.</p><p>The future outlook for the double suction pumps market appears promising.
