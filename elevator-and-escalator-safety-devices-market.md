These devices include emergency stop switches, door interlocks, overload sensors, and safety gears. Escalator Safety Devices are safety features integrated into escalators to maintain passenger safety and prevent mishaps. They include handrails with sensors, emergency stop buttons, and comb plates to prevent entrapment of objects. Both types of safety devices enhance passenger security and reduce the risk of accidents in elevators and escalators.</p></p>
