<p><p>The fiber-laser system market comprises four main types: crystal, nonlinear, rare earth doped, and plastic. Crystal fiber lasers use crystalline materials as the gain medium to generate laser light. Nonlinear fiber lasers exploit the nonlinear optical properties of the fiber to convert laser light into different wavelengths. Rare earth doped fiber lasers use rare earth elements to enhance laser performance. Finally, plastic fiber lasers utilize plastic optical fibers to transmit laser light. These various types cater to different applications and industries, offering a wide range of laser system options.</p></p>
