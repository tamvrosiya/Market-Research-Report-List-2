<p><p>Fixed panoramic sunroofs are available in two main market types: laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass sunroofs are constructed with two layers of glass bonded together with a polyvinyl butyral (PVB) interlayer, offering increased strength and better sound insulation. On the other hand, tempered glass sunroofs are made by heating the glass to a high temperature and then rapidly cooling it, resulting in increased resilience and safety. Both types offer a panoramic view and enhance the overall aesthetics of a vehicle.</p></p>
