<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Float Level Transmitter market consists of two main types: Continuous Float Level Transmitters and Magnetic Float Level Transmitters. Continuous Float Level Transmitters are designed to measure and provide continuous level readings of liquid substances in tanks or vessels. They use a float attached to a sensing rod that moves up and down with the liquid level, generating an electrical signal that corresponds to the level measurement. Magnetic Float Level Transmitters, on the other hand, utilize a float with built-in magnets to transmit level data through a magnetic field, enabling accurate level measurement without physical contact with the liquid.</p></p>
