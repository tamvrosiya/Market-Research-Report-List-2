<p><p>Forensic testing is a crucial aspect of criminal investigations. DNA profiling involves analyzing a person's genetic material to identify the culprit or establish a link between individuals and evidence. Fingerprinting analysis utilizes the unique patterns on a person's fingers to identify them or match them to crime scene evidence. Drug analysis involves testing substances to determine their composition and legality. Firearm analysis examines firearms and ammunition to link them to crimes. The "Others" category includes various types of forensic testing like toxicology analysis, handwriting analysis, and ballistics analysis.</p></p>
