<p><p>Forging presses are used in the metalworking industry to shape and mold metal. There are two main types of forging presses: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical presses use a mechanical power source, such as a rotating flywheel, to provide the force needed for forging. These presses are known for their high production rates and precision. On the other hand, hydraulic presses use hydraulic power to generate force, offering flexibility and control. They are commonly used for heavier and larger forgings. Both types of presses play a crucial role in the manufacturing process, but their specific applications and benefits vary.</p></p>
