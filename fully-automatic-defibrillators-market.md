<p><p>The fully automatic defibrillators market consists of three types: Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs), Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), and Others. AEDs are portable devices designed to analyze the heart's rhythm and deliver an electric shock to restore it to a normal rhythm. ICDs are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor and detect abnormal heart rhythms, delivering therapy as needed. The "Others" category includes wearable defibrillators, which have similar functionality as AEDs but can be worn by individuals at risk of sudden cardiac arrest.</p></p>
