<p><p>Gantry robots are classified into two types: open gantry robots and closed gantry robots. Open gantry robots have a simpler structure, with one side completely open, allowing easy access to the workspace. They are commonly used in applications such as material handling and pick-and-place operations. On the other hand, closed gantry robots have enclosed structures on all sides, providing enhanced safety and protection. They are suitable for industries where precision and containment of hazardous materials are crucial, like pharmaceutical manufacturing and cleanroom environments.</p></p>
