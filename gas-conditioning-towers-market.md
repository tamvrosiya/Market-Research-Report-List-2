<p><p>Gas conditioning towers are used in industrial processes to remove pollutants from gases before they are released into the atmosphere. There are two main types of gas conditioning towers available in the market - single nozzle system and dual nozzle system. The single nozzle system is a simpler design with one nozzle that disperses the liquid into the gas stream. On the other hand, the dual nozzle system uses two nozzles to provide a more efficient and thorough liquid dispersion. Both types provide effective gas cleaning solutions, but the dual nozzle system may offer improved performance and higher removal efficiencies.</p></p>
