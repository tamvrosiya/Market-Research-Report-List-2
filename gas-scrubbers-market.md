Dry type scrubbers, on the other hand, use dry materials like filters or sorbent beds to capture pollutants. These scrubbers are commonly used when gas streams have low moisture content or the pollutants are in particulate form. Both wet and dry type scrubbers play a crucial role in reducing industrial emissions and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
