<p><ul><li>Emulsion PCR</li><li>Bridge Amplification</li><li>Single-molecule</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene sequencing is a rapidly evolving market with various types of technologies being utilized. Emulsion PCR involves isolating individual DNA molecules in tiny water droplets and amplifying them simultaneously, making it suitable for high-throughput sequencing. Bridge amplification amplifies DNA fragments for sequencing by immobilizing them on a solid surface, enabling detection of distinct signals. Single-molecule sequencing directly sequences individual DNA molecules, eliminating the need for amplification and allowing for longer read lengths. These types of sequencing technologies are revolutionizing genetic research and enabling breakthroughs in fields like personalized medicine and biotechnology.</p></p>
