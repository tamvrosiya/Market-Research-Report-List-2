<p><p>Glass bending furnaces are used in the glass manufacturing industry to heat and bend glass into desired shapes. There are two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical type furnaces have a vertical heating chamber where the glass is placed vertically and heated from the top. These are suitable for smaller production volumes and quick production cycles. On the other hand, horizontal type furnaces have a horizontal heating chamber where the glass is placed horizontally and heated from the sides. These are suitable for larger production volumes and longer production cycles.</p></p>
