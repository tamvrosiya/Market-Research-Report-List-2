<p><p>Grout Packers are used in construction and mining industries to inject grout or cement into rock or soil formations. There are three main types of Grout Packers in the market. Mechanical type Packers are manually operated and rely on physical force to inject grout. Inflatable type Packers use compressed air or water to inflate a rubber bladder, creating a seal for grout injection. Other types of Packers include hydraulic and chemical packers that utilize hydraulic pressure or chemical reactions, respectively, to inject grout.</p></p>
