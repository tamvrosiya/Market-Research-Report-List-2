<p><p>The Gynecology Drugs market can be categorized into two main types: Hormonal Therapy and Non-hormonal Therapy. Hormonal Therapy involves the administration of medications that mimic or alter the natural hormone levels in the body, such as estrogen or progesterone. These drugs are commonly used to treat hormonal imbalances, menopausal symptoms, and conditions like endometriosis. On the other hand, Non-hormonal Therapy consists of drugs that do not affect hormone levels, but instead target specific symptoms or conditions in the female reproductive system. These can include pain medications, antibiotics, or drugs to regulate menstrual cycles.</p></p>
