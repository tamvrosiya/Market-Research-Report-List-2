The ITE hearing aid is custom-made to fit directly into the user's ear canal, while the RIC hearing aid consists of a small device placed behind the ear with a thin wire connecting to an ear tip inside the ear canal. Both these hearing aids can be designed and manufactured using 3D printing technology, allowing for personalized and comfortable solutions for individuals with hearing impairments.</p></p>
