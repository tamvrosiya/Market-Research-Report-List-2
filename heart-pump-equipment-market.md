<p><strong>The Heart Pump Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Equipment</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart pump equipment market can be categorized into two types: implantable heart pump equipment and extracorporeal heart pump equipment. Implantable heart pump equipment refers to devices that are surgically implanted inside the body to assist the heart in pumping blood. These devices are typically used for patients with severe heart failure. On the other hand, extracorporeal heart pump equipment is used outside the body to temporarily support heart function during surgeries or as a bridge to heart transplantation. These devices are connected to the patient via tubes and help maintain blood circulation during critical procedures.</p></p>
