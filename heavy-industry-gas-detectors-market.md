<p><p>Heavy Industry Gas Detectors refer to devices used to detect and monitor the presence of hazardous gases in industrial environments. These detectors are available in two types: Stationary and Portable. Stationary gas detectors are permanently installed in specific areas and provide continuous monitoring of gas levels, sending alerts when dangerous levels are detected. On the other hand, Portable gas detectors are handheld devices that can be easily carried around by workers. They are ideal for use in confined spaces or during field operations, as they provide real-time gas detection to ensure worker safety.</p></p>
