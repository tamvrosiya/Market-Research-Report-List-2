<p><strong>The Hemostasis Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Topical Hemostasis</li><li>Infusible Hemostasis</li><li>Advanced Hemostasis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemostasis products market comprises three main types: topical hemostasis, infusible hemostasis, and advanced hemostasis. Topical hemostasis refers to products applied directly to the bleeding site to promote clot formation and stop bleeding. Infusible hemostasis involves the use of intravenous or parenteral administration of clotting factors or plasma derivatives to control bleeding. Advanced hemostasis involves the use of advanced technologies, such as hemostatic agents and sealants, surgical glues, and active hemostats, to achieve hemostasis during surgical procedures or in cases of severe bleeding.</p></p>
