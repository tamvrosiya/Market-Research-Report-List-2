<p><p>High-speed aluminium foil rolling mills are classified into four types based on their functions in the production process. Universal foil rolling mills are versatile machines that can handle various foil thicknesses and widths. Roughing foil rolling mills are used for initial reduction of thick aluminium ingots into thinner coils. Intermediate foil rolling mills further reduce the thickness of the foil. Finishing foil rolling mills produce the final thin aluminum foil for various applications. Each type plays a crucial role in the efficient production of high-quality aluminium foil.</p></p>
