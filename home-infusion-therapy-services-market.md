<p><p>Home infusion therapy services market includes various types such as intravenous set, needleless catheter, and infusion pump. Intravenous set is a tube that is inserted into the vein to administer fluids or medications directly into the bloodstream. Needleless catheter is a device that allows for fluid transfer into the body without using a needle. Infusion pump is a device that delivers fluids, nutrients, or medications directly into the patient's bloodstream in a controlled and regulated manner. These technologies are commonly used in home healthcare settings to provide efficient and convenient infusion therapy services to patients.</p></p>
