<p><p>HPS (Horizontal Surface Pumps) are commonly categorized into three market types: Conventional, Electric Side Mount, and Gas Driven Side Mount. Conventional pumps are the traditional option for surface pumping applications. Electric Side Mount pumps are designed for ease of installation and efficient operation. Gas Driven Side Mount pumps offer flexibility for locations without access to electricity. The Mini Unit market consists of compact and portable pumps that are suitable for smaller scale applications or where space is limited, offering versatility and convenience.</p></p>
