<p><p>Hydraulic door closers are devices used to control the closing speed of doors, ensuring smooth and controlled shutting. Surface applied door closers are installed on the surface of the door and frame, providing easy adjustments for closing force and speed. Concealed door closers are hidden within the door or frame, offering a more aesthetic appearance. Floor springs are placed in the floor, allowing for a concealed closing mechanism which is suitable for heavy doors. These market types cater to different installation preferences and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
