<p><p>Hydronic control systems are used in heating and cooling applications to regulate the flow and temperature of fluids. The market types within this industry include actuators, valves, flow controllers, control panels, and others. Actuators are devices that convert electrical signals into mechanical motion, while valves help control the flow direction and rate. Flow controllers manage the fluid flow through pipes, and control panels provide a centralized control interface. The "others" category refers to additional components and accessories that support the functioning of a hydronic control system.</p></p>
