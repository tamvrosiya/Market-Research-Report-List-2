<p><p>Indoor smart bikes with magnetic resistance use magnets to adjust the resistance levels, providing a smooth and quiet workout experience. These bikes are highly durable and require minimal maintenance. On the other hand, indoor smart bikes with felt pad resistance use felt pads that rub against the flywheel to create resistance. While these bikes are more budget-friendly, they may require more frequent replacement of the felt pads. Both types of bikes offer a challenging workout and can be connected to apps for personalized training programs.</p></p>
