<p><p>The industrial coupling market includes various types of couplings used in industrial machinery for transmitting power and torque between two shafts. Snake spring coupling is a flexible coupling that can compensate for misalignment. Gear coupling uses interlocking teeth to transmit torque and handle high loads. Tire coupling employs rubber elements to dampen vibrations and accommodate misalignment. Diaphragm coupling uses flexible metallic diaphragms to transmit torque and handle misalignment. These couplings play crucial roles in enhancing efficiency, reducing vibrations, and ensuring reliable performance in industrial applications.</p></p>
