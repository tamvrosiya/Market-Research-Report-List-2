<p><p>Electric arc furnaces (EAC) are used in industrial settings for melting metal through the use of high-voltage electric arcs. There are two main types of EACs: AC electric arc furnaces and DC electric arc furnaces. AC EACs use alternating current to generate the electric arc, while DC EACs use direct current. Both types of furnaces are widely used in the steel industry for recycling scrap metal and producing new steel. The choice between AC and DC EACs depends on factors such as energy efficiency, cost, and production requirements.</p></p>
