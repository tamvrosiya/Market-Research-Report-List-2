<p><p>The infrared photodiode market comprises three main types: normal photodiodes, enhanced photodiodes, and others. Normal photodiodes are the standard and most commonly used type, offering basic functionalities for detecting infrared light. Enhanced photodiodes incorporate advanced technologies to improve their performance in terms of sensitivity, reduced dark current, and enhanced signal-to-noise ratio. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses a range of different types that are designed for specific applications, such as high-speed detection or photovoltaic power generation. These different types provide options for various infrared sensing needs across industries.</p></p>
