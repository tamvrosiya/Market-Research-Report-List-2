<p><p>The Inkjet Print Machine market comprises two types: Continuous Inkjet Technology and Random Inkjet Technology. Continuous Inkjet Technology is commonly used in industries for printing on various materials with faster production speeds. It involves a continuous flow of ink through small nozzles onto the printing media, controlled by a computerized system. Random Inkjet Technology, on the other hand, is often used for smaller printing applications and allows for printing on irregular surfaces. It utilizes a single printing head that moves randomly to create the desired image or text.</p></p>
