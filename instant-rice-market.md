<p><ul><li>White Rice</li><li>Brown Rice</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The instant rice market can be categorized into two main types: white rice and brown rice. White rice refers to the polished and processed version of rice, where the bran and germ are removed, leaving only the endosperm intact. It cooks quickly, has a longer shelf life, and is often preferred for its soft and fluffy texture. On the other hand, brown rice retains its bran and germ, making it more nutritious with higher fiber and nutrient content. Brown rice takes longer to cook but is often favored for its nutty flavor and health benefits.</p></p>
