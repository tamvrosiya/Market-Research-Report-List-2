<p><p>Insulation controllers are essential for maintaining the integrity of power lines by preventing electrical discharge. There are three main types of insulation controllers used in the market: suspension, line post, and braced line post. Suspension controllers are connected to overhead power lines using suspension clamps. Line post controllers are attached directly to the power line poles. Braced line post controllers have additional support for increased stability. Each type of insulation controller serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and efficiency of power distribution systems.</p></p>
