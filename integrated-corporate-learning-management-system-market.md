<p><p>The integrated corporate learning management system market includes three types: distance learning, Instructure-led training, and blended learning. Distance learning refers to the delivery of educational content remotely, allowing learners to access training materials from anywhere. Instructure-led training involves a centralized learning platform managed by a company called Instructure. Lastly, blended learning combines online and in-person training, providing a flexible learning experience that combines technology with traditional instruction methods. These market types cater to different learning preferences and offer diverse options for corporate training and development.</p></p>
