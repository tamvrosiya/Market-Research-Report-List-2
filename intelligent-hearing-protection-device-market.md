<p><p>The Intelligent Hearing Protection Device market consists of two main types: earplugs and earmuffs. Earplugs are small, flexible devices that are inserted into the ear canal to block out or reduce noise. They are portable and can be used in various environments. On the other hand, earmuffs are larger, over-the-ear devices that cover the entire outer ear to provide noise reduction. They are typically used in industrial settings or for extreme noise protection. Both earplugs and earmuffs utilize intelligent technology to actively monitor and adjust noise levels to protect the user's hearing.</p></p>
