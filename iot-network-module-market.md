<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IoT Network Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lora</li><li>NB-IoT</li><li>LTE-M</li><li>Wi-Fi</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT network module market is segmented into various types, including Lora, NB-IoT, LTE-M, Wi-Fi, and others. Lora is a low-power wide-area network suitable for long-range communication. NB-IoT is designed for low-power devices that require extended battery life and deep coverage in urban areas. LTE-M provides a combination of coverage, battery life, and scalability, ideal for IoT applications. Wi-Fi offers high-speed connectivity for devices in close proximity. The "Others" category includes various network technologies like Zigbee and Bluetooth, catering to specific IoT requirements such as low power consumption or short-range communication.</p></p>
