<p><p>Knee Ankle Foot Orthoses (KAFOs) are orthotic devices used to provide support and stability to the lower extremities. There are two types of KAFOs in the market: Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic Orthotics are designed to allow motion and flexibility at the joints, promoting natural walking and movement. They use adjustable springs or hinges to provide resistance and assistive force during gait. On the other hand, Static Orthotics are rigid and limit joint movement to correct deformities or provide stability. Both types have their specific applications based on the individual's needs.</p></p>
