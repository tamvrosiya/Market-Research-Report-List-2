<p><p>The label printer applicator machines market consists of three types: integrated automated label applicators, stand-alone labelers, and semi-automated applicators. Integrated automated label applicators are machines that can perform printing and applying labels seamlessly in a fully automated manner. Stand-alone labelers are separate machines that only apply pre-printed labels.
