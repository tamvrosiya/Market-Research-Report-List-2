<p><p>Laboratory flow cytometers are classified based on the number of channels they offer for analyzing cell samples. 6-channel flow cytometers allow for simultaneous analysis of six parameters, while 9-channel, 12-channel, and 16-channel flow cytometers offer analysis for nine, twelve, and sixteen parameters respectively. The "others" category includes flow cytometers with channel counts outside of the standard range. Each type of flow cytometer provides researchers with the ability to analyze multiple parameters of cells simultaneously, aiding in diverse research applications.</p></p>
