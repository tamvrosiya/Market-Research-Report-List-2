<p><p>Large Diameter Stainless Steel Pipes market consists of two main types: Welded Stainless Steel Pipe and Seamless Stainless Steel Pipe. Welded pipes are made by rolling a plate and welding the seam, offering cost-effective solutions for various applications. On the other hand, Seamless pipes are manufactured by piercing a solid billet, ensuring higher strength and durability. Both types of pipes are widely utilized in industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and construction due to their corrosion resistance and high performance.</p></p>
