<p><strong>The Laser Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Laser</li><li>Solid Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser cleaning system market consists of two main types: gas laser and solid laser. Gas laser cleaning systems employ a gas medium, usually carbon dioxide (CO2) or argon, to produce a laser beam for cleaning purposes. On the other hand, solid laser cleaning systems use a solid-state medium, such as a crystal or glass, to generate the laser beam. Both types of lasers are widely utilized for industrial cleaning applications, with gas lasers being more suitable for thin layers of contaminants, while solid lasers are effective for removing stubborn and thick contaminations.</p></p>
