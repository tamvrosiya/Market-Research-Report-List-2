<p><p>Laser resistor trimming systems come in two market types - X/Y Linear Drive Beam Positioning and Galvanometer Beam Positioning. The X/Y Linear Drive Beam Positioning system allows for precise trimming of resistors using a linear drive mechanism to move the laser beam along the X and Y axes. On the other hand, the Galvanometer Beam Positioning system uses galvanometer mirrors to rapidly position the laser beam with high accuracy. Both systems offer efficient and accurate resistor trimming solutions for a variety of applications.</p></p>
