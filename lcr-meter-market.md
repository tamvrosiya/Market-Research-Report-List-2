<p><p>The LCR meter market consists of two main types: handheld LCR meters and benchtop LCR meters. Handheld LCR meters are portable devices that can be easily carried and used for on-the-go measurements. They are generally smaller in size, lightweight, and suitable for field applications. On the other hand, benchtop LCR meters are larger, stationary devices designed for laboratory or industrial use. They offer higher accuracy, more advanced features, and are commonly used for detailed analysis and testing of electronic components.</p></p>
