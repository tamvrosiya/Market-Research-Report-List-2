<p><p>The light crane system market consists of two types: monorail and double-rail. The monorail light crane system employs a single rail on which the crane moves, providing flexibility and efficient usage of space. On the other hand, the double-rail light crane system utilizes two parallel rails, enabling larger load capacities and enhanced stability. Both systems are widely used in industries and warehouses where the need to move and lift heavy objects or materials efficiently and safely is paramount.</p></p>
