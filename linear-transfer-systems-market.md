<p><p>Linear transfer systems are automated systems used in manufacturing processes for transferring workpieces between different machining stations. There are two main types of linear transfer systems: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic systems utilize hydraulic power to move the workpieces along the transfer path, offering high power and precision. On the other hand, electric systems rely on electric motors for movement, which provide high speed and flexibility. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing productivity and efficiency in manufacturing industries.</p></p>
