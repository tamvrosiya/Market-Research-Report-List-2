<p><p>Liquid effluent monitors are used to measure and analyze the quality and composition of liquid discharges from industrial sites. There are two main types of liquid effluent monitors: off-line and on-line monitors. Off-line monitors require the collection of a liquid sample and subsequent analysis in a laboratory setting. On the other hand, on-line monitors provide real-time monitoring by directly analyzing the liquid stream at the discharge point. Both types of monitors play a crucial role in ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and maintaining the overall health and safety of the environment.</p></p>
