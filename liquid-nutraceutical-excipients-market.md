<p><p>The liquid nutraceutical excipients market includes various types such as binders, fillers & diluents, disintegrants, coating agents, flavoring agents, and lubricants. Disintegrants help the tablet break apart and release the ingredients upon ingestion. Coating agents provide a protective layer on the tablet. Flavoring agents add taste to the product, making it more appealing. Lubricants reduce friction between ingredients during tablet manufacturing. These excipients play crucial roles in nutraceutical formulation and delivery.</p></p>
