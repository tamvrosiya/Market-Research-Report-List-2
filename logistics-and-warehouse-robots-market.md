Parallel robots are designed to perform tasks with high speed and precision, using multiple arms attached to a fixed base. Articulated robots have several joints, allowing them to perform tasks in various directions and angles. Collaborative robots, on the other hand, are designed to work alongside humans, assisting them in tasks such as picking and packing, with safety and ease of use in mind.</p></p>
