<p><p>Low pressure bladder accumulators are used in various industries to store energy and dampen pressure fluctuations. There are three main market types for these accumulators: the welded pressure vessel market, the flexible bladder market, and the hydraulic connection market. The welded pressure vessel market involves accumulators that have a rigid shell, providing strength and durability. In the flexible bladder market, the accumulators have a flexible bladder that separates the gas and fluid, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. Lastly, the hydraulic connection market includes accumulators with a hydraulic connection for easy integration into hydraulic systems.</p></p>
