<p><p>Low Voltage Air Circuit Breakers (ACBs) are available in two main types: manually-operated and electrically-operated. Manually-operated ACBs require physical action from an operator to trip or reset the breaker, while electrically-operated ACBs can be controlled remotely using electrical signals. Manually-operated ACBs are often used in simpler systems where manual intervention is feasible, while electrically-operated ACBs are preferred in more complex systems where remote operation and automation are required for efficient functioning.</p></p>
