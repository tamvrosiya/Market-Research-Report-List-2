<p><p>Machine Vision Smart Cameras are advanced imaging devices used in industries for inspection, quality control, and process automation. There are two main types: Smart CCD Cameras and Smart CMOS Cameras. Smart CCD Cameras use Charge-Coupled Devices (CCD) as their image sensors, providing high-quality images with low noise levels. On the other hand, Smart CMOS Cameras utilize Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) technology, offering lower power consumption and faster operation. Both types offer intelligent features like image processing, pattern recognition, and communication capabilities, assisting in various applications requiring visual analysis and decision-making.</p></p>
