<p><strong>The Measle Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactivated Vaccine</li><li>Attenuated Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The measles vaccine market consists of two main types: inactivated vaccine and attenuated vaccine. Inactivated vaccines are made from killed measles virus and are considered safe for people with weakened immune systems. Attenuated vaccines, on the other hand, are made from live but weakened measles virus, which stimulates a stronger immune response. Both types of vaccines aim to prevent measles infection by triggering the production of antibodies that protect against the virus. The choice between inactivated and attenuated vaccines depends on factors such as age, overall health, and specific medical conditions of individuals.</p></p>
