<p><p>Medical bathtubs are available in both electric and manual types. Electric bathtubs are equipped with advanced features such as adjustable height, temperature control, hydrotherapy jets, and automated bathing programs. These bathtubs are preferred for patients with limited mobility as they provide enhanced comfort and convenience. On the other hand, manual bathtubs require manual effort for filling, temperature adjustment, and drainage. These bathtubs are comparatively less expensive and suitable for patients who can handle some physical effort. The market for medical bathtubs encompasses both types to cater to the varying needs and preferences of healthcare facilities and individuals.</p></p>
