<p><p>Medical disinfection and sterilization equipment market types include various methods for sterilizing medical instruments and equipment. These methods include:</p><p>1. Sterilization of UV-C Waves: This technique uses ultraviolet light of a specific wavelength to kill microorganisms and prevent their growth.</p><p>2. Ozone Sterilization: Ozone gas is used to disinfect and sterilize medical instruments by killing bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens.</p><p>3. Ethylene Oxide Sterilization: Ethylene oxide gas is used to sterilize heat-sensitive medical devices, as it efficiently kills microorganisms.</p><p>4. Pre-Vacuum Autoclave Sterilization: This method involves using high-pressure steam to sterilize medical equipment by destroying bacteria and other pathogens.</p><p>5. Cryogenic Ion Sterilization: This technique involves using ions generated from liquid nitrogen to sterilize medical instruments, ensuring their safety for use.</p><p>6. Other Methods: This market also includes other sterilization methods like steam sterilization, dry heat sterilization, and gamma irradiation, which are commonly used in healthcare settings to eliminate pathogens on medical instruments. These methods play a crucial role in preventing the spread of infections and ensuring patient safety.</p></p>
