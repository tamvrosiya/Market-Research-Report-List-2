<p><strong>The Medical Gas Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Master Alarm</li><li>Area Alarm</li><li>Local Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Gas Alarm System market encompasses three types of alarms: Master Alarm, Area Alarm, and Local Alarm. Master Alarm is a centralized monitoring system that receives signals from multiple area alarms, providing an overview of the entire facility's gas status. Area Alarm is installed in specific areas to monitor gas levels and alert staff nearby. Local Alarm is a compact alarm system designed for individual patient rooms. These alarms ensure the safety and prompt response of healthcare professionals, preventing potential gas leakage and related hazards in healthcare settings.</p></p>
