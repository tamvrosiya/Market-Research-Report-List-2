<p><p>Melamine Density Panels are commonly categorized into two main types: Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) Panels and High Density Fiberboard (HDF) Panels. MDF Panels are known for their uniform density and smooth finish, making them ideal for furniture and cabinetry. HDF Panels, on the other hand, are denser and more durable, making them suitable for applications requiring higher strength and resistance to moisture. Both MDF and HDF Panels are widely used in the construction and furniture industries due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
