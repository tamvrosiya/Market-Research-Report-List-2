<p><p>The MEMS Inertial Sensors market includes three major types of sensors: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Accelerometers measure the rate of change of velocity in an object, enabling the detection of motion, vibration, and orientation. Gyroscopes measure the angular rate of rotation, aiding in tracking the orientation and position of objects. Magnetometers measure the strength and direction of magnetic fields, assisting in determining the orientation relative to the Earth's magnetic field. These sensors find applications in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, aerospace, and healthcare, for navigation, motion sensing, and stabilization purposes.</p></p>
