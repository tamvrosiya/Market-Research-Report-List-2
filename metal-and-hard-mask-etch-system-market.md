<p><p>Metal and hard mask etch systems find application in the Front End of Line (FEOL) and Back End of Line (BEOL) processes in semiconductor manufacturing. In the FEOL, these systems are used for etching metal layers that form transistors and interconnects on the semiconductor substrate. In the BEOL, they are utilized for etching metal layers that connect different components in the integrated circuit. These etch systems are crucial for precise and accurate patterning of metal layers, ensuring the performance and functionality of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
