There are different market types of mPE, including mLLDPE (metallocene linear low-density polyethylene) and mHDPE (metallocene high-density polyethylene). mLLDPE is known for its superior toughness and puncture resistance, making it ideal for applications like packaging films. On the other hand, mHDPE is known for its stiffness and impact strength, suitable for products like industrial containers and pipes. Both types of mPE cater to specific market needs with their unique properties.</p></p>
