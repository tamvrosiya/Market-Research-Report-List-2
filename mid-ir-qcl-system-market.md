<p><p>The Mid-IR QCL System Market includes three main types of Quantum Cascade Laser (QCL) systems: Fabry-Perot QCL, Distributed Feedback QCL, and External Cavity QCL. </p><p>FP-QCL systems utilize a simple cavity design for high output power. DFB-QCL systems have a grating structure that provides single-mode emission for higher spectral purity. EC-QCL systems use an external cavity to improve wavelength tuning and beam quality. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries such as environmental monitoring, medical diagnosis, and defense.</p></p>
