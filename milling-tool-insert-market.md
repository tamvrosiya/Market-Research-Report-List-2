<p><p>The milling tool insert market encompasses various types of inserts that are used in milling operations. Carbide tool inserts are made from a combination of tungsten carbide and a binder, offering high wear resistance and toughness. CBN inserts are composed of cubic boron nitride, providing excellent heat resistance and high cutting speeds for hard materials. Ceramic inserts are made from ceramic materials, offering high heat resistance and wear resistance in high-speed machining applications. These different types of inserts cater to specific machining needs in various industries.</p></p>
