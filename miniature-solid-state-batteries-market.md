<p><p>Miniature solid state batteries are categorized into three main types: polymer-based, oxide-based, and sulfide-based. Polymer-based solid state batteries use polymer electrolytes to conduct ions, offering high flexibility and safety. Oxide-based solid state batteries utilize oxide materials as electrolytes, providing high energy density and stability. Sulfide-based solid state batteries use sulfide compounds for electrolytes, offering fast ion conduction and improved performance. Each of these battery types has unique advantages and applications in the market for miniature solid state batteries.</p></p>
