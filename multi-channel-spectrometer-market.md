<p><p>Multi-channel spectrometers are devices used to analyze light across different wavelengths. There are three main types of multi-channel spectrometers based on the range of wavelengths they cover: UV, VL, and NIR. UV spectrometers focus on ultraviolet light, VL spectrometers cover visible light, and NIR spectrometers analyze near-infrared light. Each type of spectrometer is designed to provide detailed information about the specific wavelength range it is optimized for, enabling a wide range of applications in fields such as environmental monitoring, medical diagnostics, and materials analysis.</p></p>
