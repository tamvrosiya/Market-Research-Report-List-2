<p><p>Multi-spectrum flame detectors are used in various industries to detect the presence of flames and prevent potential fires. UV flame detectors use ultraviolet sensors to detect radiation emitted by flames, making them suitable for detecting fast, tiny flames. IR flame detectors, on the other hand, use infrared sensors to detect flames based on their thermal radiation. UV & IR flame detectors combine the benefits of both UV and IR technologies to provide enhanced flame detection capability. These detectors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of buildings, industrial facilities, and public spaces.</p></p>
