<p><p>The Nephrology Drugs Market consists of different types of medications used to treat kidney-related conditions. ACE inhibitors are drugs that lower blood pressure and reduce stress on the kidneys. Calcium channel blockers also help lower blood pressure by relaxing blood vessels. Beta blockers work by reducing heart rate and blood pressure, protecting the kidneys from damage. Diuretics increase urine production, helping to remove excess fluid and waste from the body. "Others" refer to other types of medications that are used in nephrology for various purposes, such as immunosuppressants or iron supplements.</p></p>
