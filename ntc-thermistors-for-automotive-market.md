<p><p>NTC thermistors are widely used in the automotive industry for various applications. The radial type features a disc-like shape, providing a higher power dissipation capacity. The diode type combines a thermistor and a diode within a single housing, allowing temperature compensation in diode circuits. The SMD type is surface-mountable and compact, facilitating automated assembly. Other market types include glass encapsulated or probe type thermistors. These market types cater to different requirements in automotive systems, such as temperature sensing, liquid level monitoring, motor protection, and climate control, contributing to enhanced safety and efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
