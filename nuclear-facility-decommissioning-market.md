<p><p>Nuclear Facility Decommissioning Market can be categorized into three types based on the reactor technology: Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR) Decommissioning, Boiling Water Reactor (BWR) Decommissioning, and Others. PWR Decommissioning involves the dismantling and decontamination of nuclear power plants that use pressurized water as a coolant. BWR Decommissioning refers to the decommissioning of nuclear power plants that use boiling water as a coolant. The Others category includes different types of decommissioning projects, such as research reactors or experimental facilities, which do not fall under the PWR or BWR classification.</p></p>
