<p><p>The office paper shredders market is categorized into three types: strip-cut, micro-cut, and cross-cut. Strip-cut shredders cut paper into long and narrow strips, providing basic security. Micro-cut shredders offer higher security by shredding paper into tiny particles. Cross-cut shredders provide an intermediate level of security by cutting paper into small rectangular pieces. These shredders are used in offices to securely dispose of confidential or sensitive documents. Each type offers a different level of security, allowing businesses to choose the most appropriate option based on their privacy needs.</p></p>
