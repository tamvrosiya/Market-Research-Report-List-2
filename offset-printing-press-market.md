<p><p>Offset printing press is a printing method commonly used in the commercial printing industry. There are two main types of offset printing presses: sheet-fed and web-fed. Sheet-fed offset printing press involves individual sheets of paper being fed into the press, allowing for precise color and image placement. On the other hand, web-fed offset printing press uses continuous rolls of paper, enabling faster printing speed. Both types are widely used for high-quality printing in various industries, such as newspapers, magazines, catalogs, and packaging materials.</p></p>
