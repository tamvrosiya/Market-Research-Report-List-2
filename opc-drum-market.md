<p><strong>The OPC Drum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-layer Drum</li><li>Multi-layer Drum</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>OPC drum market is categorized into two types: single-layer drum and multi-layer drum. </p><p>A single-layer drum consists of a single layer of organic photosensitive material coated on a conductive base. This drum type is commonly used in low-end laser printers and fax machines due to its affordable cost.</p><p>On the other hand, a multi-layer drum comprises multiple layers of organic photosensitive materials that enhance the durability and lifespan of the drum. This type is utilized in high-end laser printers and photocopiers, as it offers better print quality, higher resolution, and longer service life compared to the single-layer drum.</p></p>
