<p><p>Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) Plants are a type of power generation system that utilizes organic fluid, such as a refrigerant or hydrocarbon, as the working medium. This cycle is based on the Rankine Cycle principle, where heat is converted into mechanical work by a turbine, and then into electricity by a generator. The use of organic fluids allows for lower boiling temperatures compared to water-based Rankine Cycle plants, which enables the utilization of low-grade heat sources, such as geothermal or waste heat.</p><p>The ORC Plants Market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for clean and sustainable energy solutions, coupled with stringent environmental regulations, is driving the market growth.
