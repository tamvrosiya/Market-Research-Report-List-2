<p><p>Paddy dryers are commonly categorized into two types: stationary and mobile. Stationary paddy dryers are built on a fixed location and are typically larger in capacity, allowing for continuous drying of large quantities of paddy. On the other hand, mobile paddy dryers are designed to be portable and can be easily moved from one location to another, allowing farmers or agricultural workers to dry paddy on-site or in different fields. Both types of paddy dryers serve the purpose of reducing moisture content in paddy for long-term storage or further processing.</p></p>
