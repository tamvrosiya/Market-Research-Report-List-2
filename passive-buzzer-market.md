<p><strong>The Passive Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary</li><li>Pogo Pin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive buzzer market types include stationary and pogo pin. Stationary passive buzzers are fixed in place and commonly found in appliances, security systems, and industrial equipment. They produce a continuous sound and are durable for long-term use. Pogo pin passive buzzers, on the other hand, use spring-loaded pins for connection. They are frequently used in portable devices like mobile phones and tablets, where space is limited. Pogo pin buzzers have the advantage of easy insertion and removal, making them convenient for testing and maintenance purposes.</p></p>
