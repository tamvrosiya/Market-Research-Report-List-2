<p><p>The personal radiation dosimeter market consists of four main types: Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescence Dosimeters (OSL), Radiophotoluminescent Dosimeters (RPL), and Active Dosimeters. TLD dosimeters use a material that emits light when heated, measuring the cumulative radiation exposure. OSL dosimeters use a material that emits light when stimulated by laser or LED, providing accurate readings for low doses. RPL dosimeters have a combination of photoluminescent and radioluminescent properties, allowing for both real-time and cumulative dose monitoring. Active dosimeters use electronic components to provide continuous, real-time monitoring of radiation levels.</p></p>
