<p><p>Personnel screening inspection systems refer to the use of advanced technologies to detect and identify potential threats or contraband items carried by individuals. The market consists of three main types: X-Ray Backscatter Imaging Technology, Millimeter Wave Imaging Technology, and Terahertz Imaging Technology. X-Ray Backscatter Imaging Technology relies on low-energy X-rays to create images of hidden objects on a person's body. Millimeter Wave Imaging Technology uses radio waves to generate detailed images, while Terahertz Imaging Technology uses electromagnetic waves to provide even greater resolution and accuracy in identifying concealed items.</p></p>
