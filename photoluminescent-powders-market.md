<p><strong>The Photoluminescent Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoluminescent powders are available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. </p><p>Water-based photoluminescent powders are formulated using water as the primary carrier. These powders are environmentally friendly, non-toxic, and easy to handle. They are often used in applications where safety and sustainability are concerns, such as in the production of signage, safety markings, and emergency exit signboards. </p><p>Solvent-based photoluminescent powders, on the other hand, use solvents as the carrier. These powders offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications that require resistance to weathering and extreme conditions. They are commonly used in the automotive, aerospace, and marine industries for purposes such as road signs, ship safety markings, and traffic signals.</p></p>
