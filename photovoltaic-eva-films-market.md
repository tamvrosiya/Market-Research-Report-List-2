<p><p>Photovoltaic EVA Films are categorized into three main types: Normal EVA Films, Anti-PID EVA Films, and Others. Normal EVA Films are standard ethylene-vinyl acetate films used for encapsulating solar cells. Anti-PID EVA Films, on the other hand, are designed to prevent potential-induced degradation in solar panels. The 'Others' category includes specialized EVA Films with unique features or applications. Each type caters to specific requirements within the photovoltaic industry, offering varied solutions for solar panel manufacturing.</p></p>
