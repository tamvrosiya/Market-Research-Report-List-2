<p><p>Pipette filters are available in various materials, with the most common types being PE, PP, PTFE, and other materials. PE filters are durable and chemically resistant, suitable for routine pipetting applications. PP filters are also chemically resistant and often used for biological and organic samples. PTFE filters are hydrophobic and ideal for filtering aggressive chemicals. Other materials offer unique properties for specific applications, such as glass fiber filters for high temperature and solvent resistance. The diversity of materials allows users to choose the best filter for their specific needs.</p></p>
