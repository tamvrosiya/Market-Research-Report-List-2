<p><p>Polystyrene Film Capacitors are electronic components used for storing and releasing electrical energy. There are two main types: Axial Polystyrene Film Capacitors and Radial Polystyrene Film Capacitors. </p><p>Axial Polystyrene Film Capacitors have two leads that are attached to each end of the capacitor, allowing for easy soldering onto a PCB. They are commonly used in applications where low capacitance values and high-precision tolerance are required.</p><p>Radial Polystyrene Film Capacitors, on the other hand, have leads that are connected to the same side of the capacitor, making them suitable for mounting onto a PCB. These capacitors are often used in applications where higher capacitance values are needed, such as filtering or coupling circuits.</p></p>
