<p><p>Power factor correction modules market types include power capacitors, AC reactors, active power filters, and others. Power capacitors are used to improve power factor by storing reactive power, while AC reactors limit electrical current to improve system performance. Active power filters compensate for harmonic distortion and improve power quality. Other types of power factor correction modules may include static VAR compensators, synchronous condensers, and dynamic voltage restorers. These devices help optimize power factor, reduce energy losses, and enhance overall system efficiency.</p></p>
