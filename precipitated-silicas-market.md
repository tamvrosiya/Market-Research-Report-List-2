<p><p>Precipitated silicas are divided into various market types based on their applications. Dental grade precipitated silica is used in dental products for polishing and cleaning teeth. Food grade precipitated silica is safe for consumption and is used as an anticaking agent, thickener, and stabilizer in food products. HDS tire grade precipitated silica enhances the performance and durability of tires by improving their grip and reducing rolling resistance.
