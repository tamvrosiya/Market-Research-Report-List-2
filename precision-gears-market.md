<p><strong>The Precision Gears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spur Gear</li><li>Helical Gear</li><li>Bevel Gear</li><li>Worm Gear</li><li>Gear Rack</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision gears market can be categorized into various types. Spur gears are the simplest and most common type, featuring straight teeth and parallel axes. Helical gears have twisted teeth and provide smoother and quieter operation. Bevel gears have intersecting axes and are used for transmitting motion between non-parallel shafts. Worm gears have a screw-like structure and offer high gear ratios. Gear racks are linear versions of spur gears and are used for converting rotary motion into linear motion. Lastly, there are other types of precision gears, such as planetary gears, spiral bevel gears, and hypoid gears, each with unique applications and characteristics.</p></p>
