<p><p>Process metal detectors are used in various industries to detect metallic contamination in products. Belt process metal detectors are integrated into conveyor belts for automatic detection. Stationary process metal detectors are fixed in a specific location for continuous monitoring. Handheld process metal detectors are portable devices for spot-checking products. Other types of process metal detectors include gravity feed, pipeline, and tablet metal detectors. Each type caters to different applications and industries, ensuring product safety and quality.</p></p>
