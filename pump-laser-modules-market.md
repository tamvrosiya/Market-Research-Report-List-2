<p><p>Pump laser modules are used in various applications, including fiber optic communications and laser pumping. The market for these modules can be categorized into two types: single-mode pump lasers and multi-mode pump lasers. Single-mode pump lasers produce laser beams with a narrow and focused output, making them suitable for long-range communication systems. On the other hand, multi-mode pump lasers produce beams with a wider output, making them ideal for short-range applications. Both types have their specific uses and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
