<p><p>Radial flow turbines are classified into two main market types: single-stage and multistage. Single-stage turbines consist of only one set of rotor blades, while multistage turbines have multiple sets of rotor blades. Single-stage turbines are typically used for applications with lower pressure ratios and simpler designs, while multistage turbines are utilized in high-pressure ratio applications that require stage-by-stage pressure reduction. The choice between single-stage and multistage turbines depends on the specific requirements of the application in terms of pressure ratios and overall performance.</p></p>
