<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT)</li><li>Brachytherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiation Oncology market encompasses different types of treatments for cancer. External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT) involves the use of a machine that delivers high-energy radiation beams to target and destroy cancer cells. It is a non-invasive treatment and is commonly used for various types of cancers. Brachytherapy, on the other hand, involves the placement of radioactive sources directly into or near the tumor site. This allows a higher dose of radiation to be delivered to the cancer cells while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues. It is typically used for cancers that are localized or in specific areas of the body.</p></p>
