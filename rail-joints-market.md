<p><p>Rail joints market consists of three main types: Standard Rail Joints, Compromise Rail Joints, Insulated Rail Joints. Standard rail joints are used to connect two rails of the same size, while compromise rail joints connect rails of different sizes. Insulated rail joints are used to isolate sections of track for signaling purposes. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring smooth and safe operation of railway systems, catering to the diverse needs of rail infrastructure projects worldwide.</p></p>
