<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The rapid thermal annealer market consists of two types: lamp-based and laser-based. Lamp-based annealers use lamps for heating the substrate, providing a uniform heat source. This type is more cost-effective and widely used in the industry. On the other hand, laser-based annealers use lasers to heat the substrate. This method offers higher precision and temperature control. However, it is more expensive and less common. Both lamp-based and laser-based annealers play significant roles in the semiconductor industry, with each type catering to different requirements and preferences.</p></p>
