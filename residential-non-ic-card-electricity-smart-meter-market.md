<p><ul><li>Single Phase</li><li>Three Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential non-IC card electricity smart meter market consists of two main types: single phase and three phase meters. Single phase meters are used in homes with a lower electricity demand and have a simpler design. They measure electricity consumption in a single circuit. On the other hand, three phase meters are used in homes with a higher electricity demand, such as those with multiple appliances or large households. These meters measure electricity consumption across three separate circuits. Both types of meters enable accurate measurement and monitoring of electricity usage in residential settings.</p></p>
