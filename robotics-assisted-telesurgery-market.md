<p><p>Robotics assisted telesurgery refers to a surgical procedure in which a surgeon performs surgery remotely using robotic technology. The surgeon operates the robot with the help of high-speed internet connectivity and advanced imaging technology, allowing them to control the robotic arms and instruments.</p><p>The robotics assisted telesurgery market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.2% during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving market growth is the increasing demand for minimally invasive surgical procedures. Robotics assisted telesurgery enables surgeons to perform complex surgeries with greater precision and reduced trauma to the patient. The technology also offers improved patient outcomes, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery times.</p><p>Another factor contributing to market growth is the advancements in robotics technology. Companies are continuously investing in research and development to enhance the capabilities of robotic surgical systems. This includes improvements in imaging technology, development of robotic instruments with better dexterity and ergonomics, and the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms.</p><p>Moreover, the growing adoption of telemedicine and telehealth services is also expected to fuel market growth. Advancements in telecommunications infrastructure and high-speed internet connectivity have made remote surgery more feasible and accessible, especially in areas with limited access to healthcare services.</p><p>In summary, the robotics assisted telesurgery market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing demand for minimally invasive procedures, advancements in robotics technology, and the growing adoption of telemedicine.
