<p><p>Safety shoes are an essential protective gear in various industries to prevent foot injuries. They come in different types based on their height, such as high-top and low-top. High-top safety shoes provide full ankle support and protection against potential hazards. They are suitable for jobs that involve heavy machinery or activities with a higher risk of ankle injuries. On the other hand, low-top safety shoes do not cover the ankles fully but still offer toe protection. They are commonly used in industries with lower ankle injury risks or jobs with more mobility requirements.</p></p>
