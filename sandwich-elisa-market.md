<p><p>Sandwich ELISA (enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay) is a diagnostic technique used to detect and quantify specific proteins in biological samples. It involves the immobilization of a capture antibody on a solid surface, which is then used to capture the target protein. After washing away any unbound protein, a detection antibody specific to the target protein is added. This detection antibody is typically conjugated with an enzyme that catalyzes a color-producing reaction, allowing for quantification of the target protein.</p><p>The Sandwich ELISA Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.80% during the forecast period.
