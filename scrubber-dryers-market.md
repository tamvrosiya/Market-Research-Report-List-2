<p><p>Scrubber-dryers refer to machines used for cleaning and drying various floor surfaces. The market for scrubber-dryers consists of two main types: walk-behind and stand-on/ride-on models. Walk-behind scrubber-dryers are operated by a person walking behind the machine, allowing for easy maneuverability in small to medium-sized areas. Stand-on/ride-on scrubber-dryers, on the other hand, provide the option for the operator to stand or ride on the machine, enabling efficient cleaning and increased productivity in larger spaces. Both types serve the purpose of effective floor cleaning and drying, but differ in terms of size and operator convenience.</p></p>
