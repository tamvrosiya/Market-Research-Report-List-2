<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Cleaning Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Wafer Etching System</li><li>Manual Wet Batch System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer cleaning equipment market includes various types of systems. The rotary wafer etching system is a fully automated equipment that uses chemicals to remove contaminants from the wafer surface. This system provides high throughput and precision cleaning. On the other hand, the manual wet batch system is a semi-automated equipment where wafers are immersed in a cleaning solution and manually agitated. This system is suitable for smaller production volumes and offers cost-effectiveness. Both systems play a critical role in ensuring the cleanliness and quality of semiconductor wafers used in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
