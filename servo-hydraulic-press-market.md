<p><strong>The Servo-Hydraulic Press Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crank Presses</li><li>Screw Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Servo-Hydraulic Press market consists of two types: Crank Presses and Screw Presses. Crank Presses utilize a connecting rod and crankshaft mechanism, converting rotary motion into linear force for the pressing process. They are suitable for high-speed and high-force applications. On the other hand, Screw Presses use a screw mechanism that produces linear motion by rotating a threaded spindle. They are known for their precise control over force and speed, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy and repeatability. Both types cater to different industrial needs and provide unique advantages in the market.</p></p>
