<p><p>Sinuscope endoscopes are medical instruments used for examining and treating sinus conditions. There are two main types: optical fiber endoscopy and electronic endoscopes. Optical fiber endoscopy utilizes flexible fibers to transmit light and images, enabling detailed visualization of the sinuses. On the other hand, electronic endoscopes utilize electronic sensors to capture and transmit images, often providing improved resolution and digital documentation. Both types play vital roles in diagnosing and treating sinus problems, but their technical mechanisms differ, offering varying benefits to healthcare professionals.</p></p>
