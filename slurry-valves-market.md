<p><p>Slurry valves are used in industries that involve the transportation of slurries, which are a combination of solid particles and liquid. Two common types of slurry valves are wafer type and full flanged type. Wafer type valves are lightweight and have a compact design, making them ideal for applications with limited space. Full flanged type valves, on the other hand, are more robust and can handle higher pressure and temperature conditions. Both types of valves play a crucial role in regulating the flow of slurries in various industries.</p></p>
