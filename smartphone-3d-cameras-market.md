<p><strong>The Smartphone 3D Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Binocular Stereo Vision</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone 3D cameras market can be categorized into two main types: Binocular Stereo Vision and Time of Flight. Binocular Stereo Vision technology uses two cameras to capture images from slightly different angles, allowing for depth perception similar to human vision. On the other hand, Time of Flight technology measures the time taken for light to bounce back from objects to determine their distance, enabling accurate 3D imaging. Both technologies aim to enhance the capabilities of smartphone cameras for capturing immersive and realistic 3D images.</p></p>
