<p><p>Solid flow rate control valves are used to regulate the flow of solid materials in various industries. Manual actuation valves are operated manually by hand, offering a basic and cost-effective option. Pneumatic actuation valves use compressed air to control the flow of solids, providing a reliable and efficient solution. Hydraulic actuation valves use hydraulic pressure to regulate solid flow, ensuring precise control. Motorized actuation valves are operated by an electric motor, offering automated and accurate control. Other types of valves include electro-pneumatic, electro-hydraulic, and solenoid valves, providing additional options for solid flow rate control.</p></p>
