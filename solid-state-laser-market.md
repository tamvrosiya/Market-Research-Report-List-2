<p><p>The Solid State Laser Market is divided into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) Lasers and Pulsed Solid State Lasers. CW Lasers emit a continuous beam of laser light and are used in various applications including laser cutting, welding, and medical treatments. On the other hand, Pulsed Solid State Lasers emit short pulses of laser light and are commonly used in applications such as material processing, marking, and scientific research. These two types cater to different market needs and have their respective advantages and applications.</p></p>
