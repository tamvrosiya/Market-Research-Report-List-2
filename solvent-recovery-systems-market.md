Centrifugal pump solvent recovery systems use centrifugal forces to separate solvents from the process stream. Vortex pump solvent recovery systems employ vortex motion to recover solvents. Other types of solvent recovery systems may include distillation units, adsorption systems, or condensation systems. These systems play a crucial role in reducing solvent waste and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
