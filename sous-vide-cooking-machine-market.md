<p><ul><li>Immersion Types</li><li>Water Bath Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sous Vide Cooking Machine market consists of two main types: immersion types and water bath types. Immersion types are compact and portable devices that are attached to a pot or container filled with water, and they maintain a precise temperature by immersing the food in this water. On the other hand, water bath types are standalone machines that have a built-in water bath. These machines maintain a precise temperature throughout the water bath to cook the food evenly. Both types offer convenient and precise sous vide cooking methods.</p></p>
