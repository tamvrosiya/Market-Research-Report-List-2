<p><strong>The Speed Logs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Doppler</li><li>Pitometer</li><li>Impeller</li><li>GPS (Global Positioning System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speed logs are devices used to measure the speed of a vessel or vehicle. The electromagnetic speed log uses electromagnetic sensors to detect water movement and calculate the speed. Doppler speed logs measure the change in frequency of sound waves reflected off particles in the water to determine speed. Pitometer speed logs use pressure gauges to measure the difference in pressure between the front and rear of a vehicle. Impeller speed logs use rotating impellers to measure water speed. GPS speed logs use satellite positioning to determine speed accurately.</p></p>
