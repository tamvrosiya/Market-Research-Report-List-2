<p><p>The spreader fertilizers machinery market encompasses various types of machinery used for spreading fertilizers on fields. Pendulum spreaders use a swinging motion to distribute fertilizers in an arc pattern. These machinery types serve the purpose of improving crop yields by accurately and efficiently applying fertilizers on fields.</p></p>
