<p><p>The stainless steel valves market comprises different types of valves, including ball valves, globe valves, butterfly valves, and others. Ball valves are commonly used for on/off applications, allowing smooth flow control. Globe valves are popular for regulating flow in a pipeline due to their excellent throttling capabilities. Butterfly valves are utilized mainly for regulating flow in large pipes, featuring a disk that rotates to control flow.
