<p><p>Studio monitors are essential for professional audio recording and mixing. There are two types of studio monitors available in the market. Active monitors, also known as powered monitors, have built-in amplifiers and do not require external amplification. They are convenient and offer precise sound reproduction. On the other hand, passive monitors rely on external amplifiers and require separate power sources. They are preferred by some audio professionals who prefer customization options and potentially higher audio quality. Both types have their own advantages, and the choice depends on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
