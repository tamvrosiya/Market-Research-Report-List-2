<p><p>Styrene Block Copolymers (SBCs) are a type of thermoplastic elastomer known for their versatility and durability. There are several types of SBCs, including Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS), Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene (SEBS), and Styrene-Ethylene-Propylene-Styrene (SEPS). SBS is commonly used in asphalt modification and footwear, while SIS is popular in adhesive applications. SEBS offers excellent UV resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for automotive and construction industries. SEPS combines the properties of SEBS and SBS, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
