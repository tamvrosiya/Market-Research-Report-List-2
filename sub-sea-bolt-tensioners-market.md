<p><p>Subsea bolt tensioners are tools used in underwater operations to tighten bolts securely. The single-hole structure tensioners are designed with one threaded opening for bolts, providing a compact and efficient solution for narrow spaces. On the other hand, double-hole structure tensioners offer more versatility with two threaded openings, allowing for simultaneous tightening of multiple bolts. Both types cater to different project requirements, providing reliable and effective solutions for various subsea applications.</p></p>
