<p><p>Submerged Arc Welders market consists of two main types: Automatic and Semi-automatic. Automatic submerged arc welders are designed to operate with minimal human intervention. They feature advanced automation technologies, such as programmable logic controllers (PLCs), which control the welding process and ensure consistent and precise welds. On the other hand, semi-automatic submerged arc welders require some degree of human involvement. Operators need to manually feed the welding wire and control the welding parameters. These welders are often preferred in situations where a higher level of control is desired or where the welding process needs to adapt to different materials or joint configurations.</p></p>
