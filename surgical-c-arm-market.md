<p><p>A Surgical C-arm is a specialized medical imaging machine that is used during surgical procedures to provide real-time, high-resolution X-ray images of the patient's anatomy. It consists of a C-shaped arm that holds the X-ray source and detector, which can be maneuvered around the patient's body to capture images from various angles. Surgical C-arms are widely used in various fields such as orthopedics, cardiovascular surgery, neurosurgery, and urology.</p><p>The Surgical C-arm Market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to do so in the coming years.
