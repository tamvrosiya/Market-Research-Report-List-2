<p><p>Switch Mode Power Supply (SMPS) transformers are used in power supplies to convert the input voltage to the desired output voltage. There are two major types: single-excited and double-excited transformers. In a single-excited transformer, the primary winding is excited by an input voltage, while in a double-excited transformer, both the primary and secondary windings are excited. Single-excited transformers are commonly used in low-power applications, while double-excited transformers are preferred for high-power applications due to their improved efficiency and lower losses.</p></p>
