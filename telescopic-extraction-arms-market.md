<p><p>Telescopic extraction arms, also known as telescopic fume extraction arms, are flexible devices used to capture and remove dust, fumes, and other airborne contaminants generated during industrial processes. These arms consist of a series of adjustable sections that can be extended or retracted to reach the desired working area. They are equipped with suction hoods at the end to capture the pollutants and a fan or extraction system that sucks the contaminants through the arm and into a filtering system.</p><p>The global telescopic extraction arms market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing focus on occupational health and safety regulations, particularly in industries such as metal fabrication, welding, and chemical processing, is driving the demand for efficient fume extraction systems. Telescopic extraction arms offer a practical and cost-effective solution to handle toxic fumes and particles, contributing to a safer working environment.</p><p>Moreover, the market growth is also influenced by the growing industrialization, increasing awareness about air pollution control, and the need for clean air in workplaces.
