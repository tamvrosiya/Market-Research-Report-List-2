<p><strong>The Three Phase Distribution Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Distribution Boards</li><li>Emergency Distribution Boards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three phase distribution boards can be categorized into two types: main distribution boards and emergency distribution boards. Main distribution boards are used to distribute electrical power from the primary source to various circuits within a building or facility. They are usually installed at the main entrance or utility room. On the other hand, emergency distribution boards are specifically designed to provide power backup during emergencies or power outages. They are typically installed in critical areas such as hospitals, data centers, and emergency response facilities. Both types of distribution boards play crucial roles in ensuring adequate power distribution and backup in different scenarios.</p></p>
