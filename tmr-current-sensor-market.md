<p><p>The TMR (Tunneling Magnetoresistance) Current Sensor market is divided into two types: closed loop and open loop. Closed loop TMR Current Sensors utilize a feedback mechanism to ensure accurate current measurement by continuously adjusting the output. This type is commonly used in applications where precise measurements are crucial, such as in power distribution systems. On the other hand, Open loop TMR Current Sensors provide a direct measurement of the current without the need for feedback, making them simpler and more cost-effective. These sensors are often used in less critical applications or where cost is a factor.</p></p>
