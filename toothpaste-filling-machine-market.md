<p><p>Toothpaste filling machines are categorized into two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic machines are equipped with advanced technology that allows them to perform all functions, from filling to capping, with limited human intervention. These machines are efficient, accurate, and have high production capacities, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual input throughout the filling process. Although they are not as fast or efficient as fully-automatic machines, they are easier to operate and more affordable, making them suitable for small to medium-sized toothpaste manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
