<p><p>Total Intravenous Anesthesia (TIVA) is a method of delivering anesthesia to patients through the intravenous route, without the use of inhaled gases. It involves the continuous administration of anesthetic drugs through a drip directly into the patient's bloodstream. TIVA offers advantages such as rapid onset and offset of anesthesia, reduced side effects, improved patient recovery, and enhanced control over the depth of anesthesia.</p><p>The Total Intravenous Anesthesia (TIVA) market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving market growth include the increasing number of surgeries worldwide, the growing preference for precise and effective anesthesia techniques, and advancements in technology. Moreover, the rising geriatric population, which is more prone to chronic diseases and surgeries, is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>In recent years, there has been a notable trend of anesthesia practitioners shifting towards TIVA techniques due to its benefits over traditional methods. TIVA provides improved hemodynamic stability, reduced post-operative nausea and vomiting, and faster recovery, leading to enhanced patient satisfaction. Additionally, advancements in drug delivery systems, such as smart infusion pumps, are further augmenting the adoption of TIVA.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has also had a positive impact on the TIVA market. The increased demand for surgeries and the need for infection control measures have prompted healthcare providers to choose TIVA over inhaled anesthesia, as it reduces the risk of viral transmission through aerosols.</p><p>Overall, the Total Intravenous Anesthesia (TIVA) market is poised for significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
