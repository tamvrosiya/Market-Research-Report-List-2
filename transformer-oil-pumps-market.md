<p><p>Transformer oil pumps are essential equipment used in power transformers to ensure efficient cooling and proper functioning of the transformer. There are two main types of transformer oil pumps: centrifugal and axial flow pumps. </p><p>Centrifugal pumps work by converting rotational energy into fluid energy, creating a centrifugal force that moves the oil. These pumps are reliable and widely used, providing steady flow rates.</p><p>Axial flow pumps, on the other hand, use propeller-like blades to push the oil axially through the pump. They are preferred in situations where a large volume of oil needs to be moved at low pressure.</p><p>Both types of pumps play crucial roles in maintaining optimal temperature and performance in power transformers.</p></p>
