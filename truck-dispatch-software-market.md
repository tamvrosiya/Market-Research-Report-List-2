<p><p>Truck dispatch software is available in two market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers advantages like scalability, flexibility, and easy accessibility from multiple devices. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed locally on a company's servers and operates within their own infrastructure. It provides complete control over the software and data but may require higher upfront costs and maintenance. Both market types cater to the needs of trucking companies but differ in terms of deployment and management.</p></p>
