<p><p>Ultraviolet-Visible Spectrometer market consists of two main types: Single Beam and Double Beam. Single Beam spectrometers utilize a single light source and detector, providing simpler operation and lower cost. On the other hand, Double Beam spectrometers use two light beams, one passing through the sample and the other through a reference substance, allowing for more accurate and reliable measurements. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and academic research.</p></p>
