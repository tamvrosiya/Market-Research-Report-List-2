<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The underground ventilation system market consists of three types: axial flow fans system, centrifugal fans system, and mixed flow fans system. Axial flow fans are suitable for low resistance and high flow rate scenarios, commonly used in mines and tunnels. Centrifugal fans are ideal for situations requiring higher pressure, typically found in parking garages and subway systems. Mixed flow fans combine the characteristics of both axial and centrifugal fans, providing a balance between pressure and flow, often used in industrial settings.</p></p>
