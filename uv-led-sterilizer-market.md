UV-B LEDs emit medium-wave ultraviolet light and are utilized in medical therapy, sunbeds, and printing applications. UV-C LEDs emit short-wave ultraviolet light and are highly effective in sterilization and disinfection processes, finding applications in water and air purification systems, as well as surface sterilization in healthcare facilities and consumer products.</p></p>
