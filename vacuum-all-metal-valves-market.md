<p><p>Vacuum All-Metal Valves are used in highly specialized applications where extreme conditions such as high or ultra-high vacuum are required. XHV (Extra High Vacuum) Valves are designed to operate in vacuum environments with a pressure range of 10^-12 mbar, while UHV (Ultra High Vacuum) Valves are capable of maintaining pressure levels as low as 10^-9 mbar. These valves ensure efficient sealing and minimal outgassing in order to maintain the integrity of the vacuum system. Both XHV and UHV Valves play crucial roles in various scientific and industrial applications that necessitate the use of ultra-clean and uncontaminated environments.</p></p>
