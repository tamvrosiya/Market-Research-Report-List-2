<p><p>The vacuum ejectors market is segmented into two main types: single stage and multi-stage vacuum ejectors. Single stage vacuum ejectors operate by using a high-pressure motive fluid to create a vacuum. They are commonly used for simple applications where the required vacuum level is not very high. On the other hand, multi-stage vacuum ejectors employ multiple stages of operation to achieve higher vacuum levels. They are utilized in more complex applications that require a deeper vacuum.</p></p>
