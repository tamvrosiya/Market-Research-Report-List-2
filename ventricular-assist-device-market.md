<p><strong>The Ventricular Assist Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LVADs</li><li>RVADs</li><li>BIVADs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ventricular Assist Device (VAD) market consists of three main types: LVADs, RVADs, and BIVADs. LVADs, or Left Ventricular Assist Devices, are surgically implanted in the left side of the heart and help pump blood from the left ventricle to the aorta, aiding heart function. RVADs, or Right Ventricular Assist Devices, provide support to the right side of the heart, pumping blood from the right ventricle to the pulmonary artery. BIVADs, or Biventricular Assist Devices, support both sides of the heart, aiding both left and right ventricles in circulating blood.</p></p>
