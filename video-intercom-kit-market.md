<p><p>Video intercom kits are available in two different market types: analog type and IP type. Analog type kits connect to a dedicated analog intercom system, using traditional wiring methods for signal transmission. They are reliable but limited in terms of advanced features. On the other hand, IP type kits utilize internet protocol technology, allowing them to connect to the network and offer more advanced features such as remote access and integration with other smart devices. IP type kits require a stable internet connection for optimal performance.</p></p>
