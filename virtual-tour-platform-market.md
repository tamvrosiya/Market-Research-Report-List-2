<p><p>A virtual tour platform is a software or application that allows users to explore and experience a location or property virtually, without being physically present. The increasing demand for virtual tours in various industries, such as real estate, hospitality, tourism, and education, is a major factor driving this growth.</p><p>In the real estate industry, virtual tour platforms are being widely used to showcase properties to potential buyers or tenants. It allows them to explore the property remotely and get a realistic feel for the space before making any physical visits. This not only saves time and effort but also improves the overall buying or renting experience.</p><p>The hospitality and tourism sectors are also utilizing virtual tour platforms to provide immersive experiences to their customers. Hotels, resorts, and tourist attractions are creating virtual tours that allow travelers to preview the facilities and attractions before making bookings or planning visits.</p><p>Virtual tour platforms are also being embraced by educational institutions to offer virtual campus tours, making it easier for prospective students to explore the facilities and get a sense of the campus culture. This trend has gained even more momentum during the COVID-19 pandemic, as physical campus visits became restricted.</p><p>Overall, the virtual tour platform market is witnessing rapid growth due to its ability to provide immersive and interactive experiences in various industries. The market is expected to continue expanding as more businesses and organizations realize the benefits of incorporating virtual tours into their offerings.</p></p>

<p><p>The virtual tour platform market can be divided into two types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based platforms store all data and processing power in remote servers, allowing users to access and interact with virtual tours from any device with internet access. These platforms offer flexibility and scalability, as they can handle large amounts of data and user traffic. In contrast, web-based platforms run directly on a web browser, eliminating the need for additional installations. They offer convenience and ease of use, as users can view virtual tours instantly without downloading any software.</p></p>
