<p><p>Voltage converters market can be categorized into three types: low voltage converters, medium voltage converters, and high voltage converters. Low voltage converters are designed to convert voltages below 1000 volts, commonly used in residential and commercial applications. Medium voltage converters deal with voltages between 1000 and 35,000 volts, commonly used in industrial settings. High voltage converters are designed to handle voltages above 35,000 volts, typically used in utility power systems and large-scale industrial applications. Different types of voltage converters cater to specific voltage requirements in different sectors.</p></p>
