Vacuum wave making systems, on the other hand, create waves by removing air or water from an enclosed space, causing a pressure difference. The "Others" category encompasses alternative wave making system technologies that may employ different mechanisms to produce waves, such as mechanical or electromagnetic systems. These different market types cater to diverse needs and preferences in creating wave effects.</p></p>
