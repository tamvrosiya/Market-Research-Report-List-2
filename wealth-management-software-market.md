<p><p>Wealth management software market has two main types, namely cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers increased scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness as data is stored and processed offsite. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed on a company's local servers and infrastructure. It provides higher control and customization options but requires internal maintenance and IT support. Both options have their own advantages and considerations, and companies choose based on their specific needs, budget, and security requirements.</p></p>
