<p><p>WLAN (Wireless Local Area Network) front-end modules are components used in wireless communication devices to enhance signal transmission and reception capabilities. The market for these modules can be categorized into different types. Power Amplifiers (PA) are responsible for increasing the power of the signal, while Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA) minimize the noise in the received signal. Switches facilitate the selection and routing of signals within the device. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses additional components such as filters, duplexers, and diplexers that play a role in optimizing the overall performance of WLAN devices.</p></p>
