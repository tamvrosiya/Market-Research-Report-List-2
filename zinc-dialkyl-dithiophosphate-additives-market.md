<p><p>Zinc Dialkyl Dithiophosphate (ZDDP) additives are commonly used in lubricants to improve their anti-wear and antioxidation properties. There are two main types of ZDDP additives market: Primary ZDDP additives and Secondary ZDDP additives. Primary ZDDP additives are directly added to the lubricant during manufacturing, while Secondary ZDDP additives are mixed with the lubricants during usage. Primary additives generally offer better performance but are more expensive, whereas Secondary additives are more cost-effective but may not provide as strong of a protective barrier.</p></p>
